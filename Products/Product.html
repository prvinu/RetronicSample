Retronics Products

Retronics provides a full variety of comprehensive products and solutions for a wide range of vertical sectors thanks to its substantial and highly experienced R&D team.

Smart Access

Smart Access is a solution that allows access control without the need for any keys or cards. It works with the user’s mobile device and is designed for it to be very simple to operate. When the application is opened, the user will be asked for his/her biometrics and upon confirmation will grant access in a split second.
