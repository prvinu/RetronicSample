About us:

Our Vision: “To establish Retronics as a world leader in the field of Retail Automation.”

Our Mission: “To innovate and help our customers in their innovations.”

Retronics or Retronic Systems is an organization which intends to design and develop systems that are focussed on automation in the retail sector. We understand that this industry faces the consumer directly and the experience of the consumer is paramount for any innovation.

To cater to this demand, at Retronics, we have a team of experienced professionals, who have worked on industry first designs in this sector. With a collective experience of over 50 years, we are focussed on bringing in innovative technology and providing unparalleled support to our customers.
